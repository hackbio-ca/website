We are Toronto's leading bioinformatics hackathon, bringing together early career scientists and students from the Discovery District to tackle interdisciplinary projects across bioinformatics, biology, statistics, and computer science, with a focus on computational and entrepreneurial thinking.

In partnership with the Terrence Donnelly Centre and the Temerty Faculty of Medicine's Computational Biology in Molecular Genetics program, participants collaborate to develop solutions to key challenges in computational biology.

Our first event was held in September 2024, with platinum sponsorships from Deep Genomics and the Donnelly Centre. The hackathon brought together over 70 early-career trainees from diverse STEM backgrounds, who completed 12 bioinformatics projects over a three-day period (DevPost, GitHub). The event included catered meals, social programming, networking sessions, and an overnight component. Projects were judged on scientific and entrepreneurial merit. Top teams and individuals received awards. View the 2024 photo album here.
